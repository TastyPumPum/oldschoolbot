title: "Lucky Pick"
---

Lucky Pick is a one-pick mystery-button gamble.

Start with:
[[/gamble lucky_pick amount\:100m]]

## Bet Limits

- Minimum bet: 1m GP
- Maximum bet: 3b GP

## Rules

- You must confirm the bet before the pick.
- You pick **one** mystery button.
- After selection, all button values are revealed.
- Possible outcomes include 0x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 5x, and 10x.
- If you do not pick in time, your bet is refunded.

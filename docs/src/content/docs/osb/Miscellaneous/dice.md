---
title: "Dice"
---

Dice is a simple percentile roll gamble.

## Commands

- Free roll: [[/gamble dice]]
- GP gamble: [[/gamble dice amount\:100m]]

## Rules

- Roll is from 1 to 100.
- You win if the roll is **55 or higher**.
- On win, you gain your bet amount.
- On loss, you lose your bet amount.

## Bet Limits

- Minimum bet: 1m GP
- Maximum bet: 500m GP
